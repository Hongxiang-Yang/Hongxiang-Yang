Welcome to my Meta-Reality

“I’m a boy from a coastal city in China. During my college years, I majored in media studies. Later, I crossed oceans to pursue my master’s degree in London. Throughout this journey, I delved deep into digital creative media, particularly in virtual social interactions.

After three years of work experience, I reevaluated my passions and focused on researching Social VR, aiming to enhance people’s engagement in future virtual social experiences. I believe in empowering individuals to cultivate unique identities and foster more meaningful social interactions in virtual worlds.”
